Swipe-Survival Test

This is the real test. It schedules a notification inside the service worker itself (not the page), then you fully swipe the app away. If it still fires, your OS keeps the service worker alive that long. If not, that's the exact point your OS kills it — which is the real cause of missed push notifications, not your code.
